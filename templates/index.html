Diabetes and You

How Diabetes affects the human body

Diabetes is a disease that afffects the way your body hadnles glucose. Sugar that provides energy to your cells.

When you consume food, glucose is then absorbed by your intestines, which is then distributed to the cells in your body via your bloodstream. Currently there are two types of Diabetes. Type 1 and Type 2.
